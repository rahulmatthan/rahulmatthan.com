The settlement gap

A trade and its payment sit on two different ledgers today, so settlement takes a day. That gap is not free — money moved for a trade that has not yet settled is locked, earning nothing for anyone. Put your own numbers in and see what the gap costs.

Under tokenised settlement The asset and the money change hands in the same atomic operation. The float is ₹0. The ₹35 crore goes back to your balance sheet.
