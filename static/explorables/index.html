Small interactive explainers that accompany my Ex Machina column — one concept, one parameter, one insight you can feel by trying it.

  • Ex Machina 475 · The Quantum Reckoning
    How hard is factoring?
    RSA hides its secret inside a number that is the product of two very large primes.
  • Ex Machina 474 · A Matter of Sovereignty
    The patch deficit
    Security teams fix vulnerabilities at a roughly fixed rate — a well-resourced team can clear maybe five critical bugs a day.
  • Ex Machina 473 · The Digital Media Amendments
    The collapsing appeal
    India's IT Rules today route a takedown complaint through three stages: the platform's own Grievance Officer, then the Grievance Appellate Committee, then the Inter-Departmental Committee at the top.
  • Harvest now, decrypt later
    Adversaries are already collecting encrypted traffic they cannot yet read, in the hope of decrypting it when quantum computers arrive.
