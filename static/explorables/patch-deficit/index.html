The patch deficit

Security teams fix vulnerabilities at a roughly fixed rate — a well-resourced team can clear maybe five critical bugs a day. What just changed is the other side of the equation: AI-assisted tools can surface new vulnerabilities faster than any team can possibly patch them. The gap between what is found and what is fixed is the number of known bugs that simply sit open, waiting.
