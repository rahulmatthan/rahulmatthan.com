Deepak Disagrees is a different type of podcast in which the host (Deepak VS) takes a topic the guest feels strongly about and debates him or her on it. There aren't a whole lot of things I feel that strongly about, but I thought that ownership of property - and in particular intellectual property - would be a good subject for debate. This is a wide-ranging discussion on a host of issues I do not often get the opportunity to discuss.
