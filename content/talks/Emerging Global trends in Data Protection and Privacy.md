At SAMVAAD 2024, I participated in a panel discussion that examined the evolving landscape of global data protection and privacy regulations, with a focus on some recent trends and their impact across various industries. The panel was moderated by Venkatesh Hariharan and included Chanchal Sarkar from the Department of Economic Affairs, Mathew Chacko from SpiceRoute Legal and Nehaa Chaudhari from Ikigai Law
